CATS

Big Floofy Cats

I never thought I was a "cat person" but when my partner and I first moved into our house I really wanted a pet. I was leaning towards a dog but he insisted cats were low maintenance and would be a good first pet for us. We now have two big floof balls that I couldn't love more. They have such distinct personalities, they shed as much as a dog would but they don't jump on people! They're both part Maine Coon which means they are just bigger than your average house cat and have a few funny quirks. Darwin loves water! The second you head towards the bathtub suddenly its a race that he is determined to win. Mortimer is incredibly shy, when we first brought him home he mostly just hid under our cabinets and would never let you get too close for pets or cuddles. After a lot of effort and patience he has learnt that he secretly likes to cuddle, as long as its on his terms!
